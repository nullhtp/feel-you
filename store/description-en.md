## Full Description

Feel You is an accessibility app that teaches Morse code through vibration and touch — no sight or hearing needed.

Designed for deaf-blind individuals, Feel You turns your phone into a patient, personal Morse code teacher. The app vibrates each character's Morse pattern and waits for you to tap it back. Take as long as you need — there are no timers, no quizzes, and no pressure.

**How it works:**
- The app vibrates a Morse code pattern for a character
- Tap the screen to reproduce the pattern (short tap = dot, long tap = dash)
- Get instant vibration feedback: three quick pulses for correct, one long buzz for incorrect
- The pattern repeats patiently until you get it right
- Move at your own pace through digits, letters, and words

**Features:**
- Full Morse code curriculum: digits (0-9), English letters (A-Z), and Arabic letters
- Vibration-only feedback — works without seeing or hearing the screen
- Intuitive gesture navigation: swipe to move between characters, shake to go home
- Landscape orientation for comfortable two-handed use
- Companion overlay for sighted helpers to follow along
- No ads, no in-app purchases, no data collection

**Supported languages:**
- English (International Morse Code)
- Arabic (Arabic Morse Code)

Feel You is more than a learning tool — it's the first step toward independent communication for deaf-blind people through a device they already carry.
